Suggested Accomodations for Guests (Request UCSF Rates):

  • Harbour Court Hotel
    • District: Embarcadero (10 minutes driving to Mission Bay).
    Rates: $210.00 and up plus hotel taxes.
    Remarks: Convenient for Mission Bay guests.
  • Hotel Griffon
    • District: Embarcadero (In between Mission Bay Campus and Fisherman's Wharf).
    Rates: $175 and up.
  • Stanyan Park Hotel
    • District: Haight Ashbury (walking distance to Parnassus Campus).
    Rates: $140.00 for standard room (UCSF negotiated rate) (direct billing).
  • Inn 1890 Bed and Breakfast
    • District: Haight Ashbury (walking distance to Parnassus Campus).
    Rates: $89.00-$159.00 plus hotel taxes.
    Remarks: Breakfast included. Relaxed and friendly environment.
  • The Harper House
    • District: Haight Ashbury (walking distance to Parnassus Campus).
    Remarks: The Harper House rents apartments monthly. Furnished rooms. Great for short term visitors.

  • Hyatt Regency San Francisco Airport
