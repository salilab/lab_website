Getting to RU Campus

Rockefeller University is located on the east side of York Avenue and 66th Street in Manhattan. If arriving by plane or train, the campus is most easily and directly reached by taxi cab from Kennedy, LaGuardia, or Newark Airport, or from Pennsylvania or Grand Central Stations. The closest subway is the 59th street station on Third Avenue, where the Lexington Avenue local (IRT) #6, N and R trains stop.
